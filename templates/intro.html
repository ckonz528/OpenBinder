Open Book Features

You can upload notes that you have taken electronically to our site for storage and managing. We support image files, word documents, excel notebooks, and PDFs.
Uploaded notes can be tagged with words or phrases describing the content, and these tags can be used along with the filename to search and organize your notes.
Your uploaded notes can be shared with all other users of the site, so now you can collaborate to take notes, study material for a class, or prepare for an exam!
